# Vietnam Education Policy Impact Analysis
**Socio-economic analysis regarding the integration of TOPIK in the National Curriculum.**

### Objective
To quantify the impact of recognizing TOPIK as a national credit on high school graduation rates and university entrance efficacy for 1.1M+ Vietnamese students.

### Tools Used
* **Python:** Data cleaning and multivariate regression.
* **Pandas & NumPy:** For processing large-scale demographic datasets.
* **Matplotlib/Seaborn:** For legislative impact visualizations.

### Key Findings
* Identified a [X]% correlation between language certification and higher-tier university placement.
* Strategic data visualizations contributed to the Jan 2026 MoET Ministerial Decree.

> *Note: This repository contains the methodology and cleaned visualizations used in the policy research.*
